This data plot was inspired by the work of Aatish Bhatia. This plot shows how much each state in india is following the trendline during the Coronavirus Pandemic in 2020. As in Aatish Bhatia's plot at (https://aatishb.com/covidtrends/) it is the plot of total confirmed cases verses new confirmed cases in the past week.
