The Parabolic Constant Mystery

Manas Shetty, Prajwal DSouza, Sparsha Kumari, Vinton Adrian Rebello

Let us start with a innocent looking problem. Imagine a unit square, a perfect little 1x1 entity. Now, pick a point Q at random along the square's boundary. What do you think is the average distance between this chosen point Q and the very heart of our square, the center C?
